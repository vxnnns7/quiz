Quiz Instructions

  1. Click the "Play" button to begin the quiz.

  3. Read each question carefully and select the option you believe is correct.
  4. Your score will be displayed at the end

  6. At the end of the quiz, your score will be displayed along with feedback on how well you did.
  7. Enjoy the quiz and have fun testing your programming language knowledge!
